# VISION.md

## North Star
Ascension Earth should feel like a clear, mystical, high-agency digital card experience where every turn is legible, meaningful, and emotionally tense.

## Platform Intent
- Ship as a browser-first game that is quick to load, easy to replay, and simple to iterate.
- Preserve deterministic seeds so matches are reproducible for tuning and playtesting.
- Favor AI-generated or procedural assets so visual/audio quality can improve without manual pipelines.

## Experience Pillars
- Clarity first: players always know what to do next.
- Strategic depth: card and spell choices create real tradeoffs.
- Polished feel: feedback, pacing, and transitions support immersion.
